How It Works

What is SwiftAid? Press for Help is a direct line to emergency services. This system ensures that help is dispatched as quickly as possible to assist you in a crisis.

When you press for help, your request is routed to a trained dispatcher. The dispatcher will gather information from you, assess your situation, and send the appropriate assistance based on your location and the nature of the emergency.
